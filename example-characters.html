👥 Example Player Characters

These eight fully-built characters demonstrate every mechanic in the Reality RPG ruleset — ethnicity, culture, mental health, socioeconomic status, personality traits, skills, daily routines, and more. Use them as templates, inspiration, or ready-to-play characters.

💡 How to Use These Characters

Each character below is a complete starting PC. Copy them directly into a campaign, or use them as reference when building your own. The attribute modifiers follow the core rules: floor(Attribute ÷ 5). All skill levels are per the character creation guidelines for each age bracket.

⚖️ Content Note

These characters include real mental health conditions, socioeconomic struggles, and trauma histories. Before using them at your table, review Safety Tools and establish Lines and Veils with your group.

1. Mei Lin Chen, 23 🇨🇳🇺🇸

Mei Lin Chen
23 · East Asian American · Software Engineering Intern · San Francisco, CA
🏋️ VIG 8 (+1) 🧠 INT 14 (+2) 💬 EXP 7 (+1) 🎯 DXT 9 (+1) 🛡️ RES 6 (+1) ✨ PRC 8 (+1)
📖 Backstory

Mei grew up in San Jose in a household where love was expressed through homework help and college savings plans. Her parents, immigrants from Guangdong, worked double shifts at a hospital and a restaurant so she could have "the life they couldn't." She never questioned the sacrifice — she just studied harder. Now she's a junior at UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science, interning at a mid-size tech startup in SOMA, and living in her first studio apartment. She's proud of how far she's come, but the anxiety that's been her constant companion since high school hasn't gotten the memo that she's supposed to be winning at life now.

6. Tallulah Red Elk, 38 🌿🦅

Tallulah Red Elk
38 · Native American (Lakota) · Cultural Liaison Worker · Pine Ridge, SD / Rapid City
🏋️ VIG 11 (+2) 🧠 INT 10 (+2) 💬 EXP 14 (+2) 🎯 DXT 9 (+1) 🛡️ RES 14 (+2) ✨ PRC 11 (+2)
📖 Backstory

Tallulah was raised on the Pine Ridge Reservation by her grandmother after her mother — a survivor of the Indian boarding school system — couldn't provide a stable home.
